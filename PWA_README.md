4. The app will appear in your applications and can be launched like a native app

#### On Mobile (Android):
1. Open the app on your phone's browser
2. The install prompt will appear automatically
3. Tap "INSTALL NOW" to add to home screen
4. The app icon will appear on your home screen

#### On iOS (Safari):
iOS doesn't support the `beforeinstallprompt` event, but users can still install:
1. Open the app in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Select "Add to Home Screen"
4. The app will be added with the custom icon and branding

The PWA is configured in `vite.config.ts` with:
- **Service Worker**: Automatically generated by Workbox
- **Caching Strategy**: 
  - Google Fonts: Cache-first (1 year)
  - Tailwind CSS: Cache-first (30 days)
  - All assets: Pre-cached for offline use
- **Update Strategy**: Prompt mode (users are notified of updates)
